FAQ

Our tours vary in length, ranging from half-day excursions to multi-day adventures. Most of our standard tours are designed to last between 1 to 7 days, allowing you to choose the experience that best fits your schedule.

Meal inclusions vary by tour package. Some packages include meals, while others do not. Detailed information regarding meal inclusions will be provided in the tour description. Please check the specific package for more information.

Our cancellation policy is designed to offer flexibility while ensuring operational efficiency. Our cancellation policy allows you to cancel your booking up to 48 hours before the tour for a full refund. If you cancel within 48 hours, a cancellation fee may apply. Please check the specific terms outlined during the booking process for your selected tour.

We recommend packing light and bringing essentials based on your destination. Essentials typically include comfortable clothing, sturdy shoes, toiletries, sunscreen, and any personal medications. If your tour includes specific activities (like hiking or swimming), be sure to pack accordingly!
